Voice & Tone

Language Standards

  • UK English spelling throughout (colour, optimise, behaviour)
  • No contractions in formal copy (write "do not" not "don't")
  • Professional but approachable - expert without being condescending
  • Direct and confident - avoid hedging language

Writing Guidelines

Do

  • Use active voice
  • Be specific with outcomes
  • Lead with benefits
  • Use "you" and "your"
  • Keep sentences clear

Avoid

  • Unexplained jargon
  • Hyperbole or exaggeration
  • Passive constructions
  • Unnecessary adjectives
  • Exclamation marks

Key Terminology

Term Usage
12 Growth Accelerators Core framework - always capitalised
Constraint Zone Where businesses are stuck - always capitalised
Growth Zone Target state - always capitalised

Logo & Identity

Logo Usage

  • The logo is "Quentin Hunter" in Merriweather font
  • Primary colour: Teal (#0d9488)
  • On dark backgrounds: White (#ffffff)
  • Minimum size: 120px width for digital

Logo Don'ts

Never

  • Change the font
  • Stretch or distort
  • Use non-brand colours
  • Add effects (shadows, gradients)
  • Place on busy backgrounds

Always

  • Maintain clear space
  • Use approved colours only
  • Ensure sufficient contrast
  • Keep proportions intact
  • Use high-resolution files
