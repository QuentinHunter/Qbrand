Cookie Policy

Last updated: 22 December 2025

What Are Cookies?

A cookie is a small piece of data that a website stores on your device when you visit. Cookies enable features like logging in, track usage through analytics, store user preferences, and personalise content.

Cookie Categories

First-Party vs. Third-Party: First-party cookies track activity on quentinhunter.com specifically. Third-party cookies, set by other companies, may track users across multiple websites using the same service.

Essential Cookies

These are necessary for core website functions like user logins, account management, and form submissions. Without these cookies, the website cannot function properly.

Performance Cookies

Performance cookies track how you use the website during your visit. Data collected is typically anonymous and aggregated across users, helping us understand visitor patterns and improve the site experience.

Functionality Cookies

These collect information about your device and user-configured settings (language, time zone). They enable customised and optimised content delivery.

Targeting/Advertising Cookies

These determine relevant promotional content based on your interests and limit advertisement frequency. Third-party versions may track users across other websites using the same service. We use targeting and advertising cookies to serve relevant content.

Managing Cookies

You can instruct your browser to refuse cookies, though this may limit website functionality. Most browsers allow you to:

  • See what cookies are stored and delete them individually
  • Block third-party cookies
  • Block cookies from specific sites
  • Block all cookies
  • Delete all cookies when you close your browser
